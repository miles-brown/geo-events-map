# Real Unusual London Events Research

## Events Identified

### 1. London Beer Flood (1814)
- **Date**: October 17, 1814
- **Location**: St Giles, Tottenham Court Road area
- **Coordinates**: 51.5165, -0.1308 (Great Russell Street/Tottenham Court Road)
- **Description**: Horse Shoe Brewery tank ruptured, releasing 320,000 gallons of beer in a 15-foot wave
- **Deaths**: 8 people killed
- **Category**: accident
- **Crime**: false
- **Video search**: "London Beer Flood 1814"
